Benefits

Here are the benefits that my service is designed to produce...

  • Focus on creating an awesome program without worrying about how you're going to deliver it
  • Set program content website up very quickly and easily
  • Add new programs quickly and easily
  • Provide life-time access to program content to your clients
  • Build community around your program
  • Run multiple programs in parallel (e.g. Program A, Program B, Maintenance Program)
  • Each program participant gets an unique password. This means that you don't have to maintain a single password that you have to rotate from time to time.
  • Single password to get access to both program content and discussion forum - your clients don't need a separate login for discussion forum. Everything is under one roof.
  • Save time helping your clients if they forgot their password. Your clients will be able to reset their password themselves.
  • I'm going to be your technical consultant. You will get a lot of technical support and hand holding from me.

Why it is better than a private page with a password

  • If you have a page protected with one master password you have to rotate that password from time to time.
  • If someone decides to leave your program you have no good way of stopping him from accessing your program content.

Why it is better than Wordpress membership plugin

  • Easier to set up and get started
  • Technical support - it's an essential part of this service
  • No need to worry about hosting - this service takes care of it

Why it is better than private Facebook group

  • Build your brand - your discussion forum looks similar to your website
  • Save time adding clients to the program - no need for an extra step to add them to the private Facebook group
  • Your clients can put information in their profiles that they wouldn't put in their Facebook profiles
  • Your clients no longer are exposed to Facebook ads when they participate in discussion
